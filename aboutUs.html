Our Cinemas sets new benchmarks for entertainment in Jordan

lifestyle center announced that Cinemas, the largest complex in Jordan, has officially opened its doors to the public setting a new benchmark for entertainment in Jordan. Located on Terrace 2, MARVEL Cinemas is spread over 7000 m² and seats a total of 1700 people through its 16 theatre multiplex that includes 12 regular theatres and 4 VIP halls. MARVEL Cinemas is the first to introduce the latest in high-tech services to movie-goers such as an online ticketing function to avoid queues, e-kiosks to purchase movie tickets with credit or debit cards, and a mobile application for purchasing movie tickets and to gain access to everything happening at the cinema. Ibrahim Taher, CEO of MARVEL Cinemas, stated, “We are very proud to see MARVEL Cinemas successfully open to the Jordanian public. We have worked hard to ensure that we launch a product that will set a new precedent for entertainment in Jordan. We look forward to becoming the number one movie destination in Jordan, and we believe that through our exclusive products and unique cinema experience, we will be able to achieve that goal.” With the launch of MARVEL Cinemas, the largest cinema complex to ever open in Jordan, we will be able to offer our customers a truly unparalleled lifestyle that will take their movie experience to the next level. MARVEL Cinemas is also introducing the very first VIP theatres, better known as MARVEL Class, that have a capacity of 113 VIP seats with fully reclining leather armchairs and butler service. All four VIP theatres are equipped with the latest in cutting-edge technology such as 4K projectors capable of a dazzling 8 million megapixel and the latest cinema sound conception , which is also a first in Jordan. MARVEL Cinemas is the result of a partnership between three leaders in the entertainment industry in the Middle East which are Al Taher for Cinemas, Eagle Films and Selim Ramia & Co.
