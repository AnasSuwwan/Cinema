Cinema CLASS

The very first VIP Theater in Amman can only be at the largest Cinema complex in Jordan. In addition to the 12 regular theaters, MARVEL Cinemas boasts 4 VIP halls, the MARVEL CLASS., with a total capacity of 100 seats, all of which are fully reclining for more comfort and luxury. The five star treatments include your own butler service straight at your seat; all you need to do is press the buzzer and he will be at your service.

REAL D 3D

The world's leading digital 3D experience provider, RealD, has brought the premier digital 3D experience to cinemas worldwide. MARVEL Cinemas is delighted to present the best movie-going experience with RealD 3D. The new generation of entertainment offers intense, ultra-realistic images that transform you straight into the movie. Get inside the action, take a voyage into uncharted lands, make friends with Hollywood’s favorites, all in RealD 3D. In addition to the realistic imaging you will also not miss a single hiss with RealD 3D crystal-clear digital surround sound. Don’t just watch a movie… experience it!

MOBILE APPLICATION

Not only is MARVEL Cinemas online but on your mobile phone as well. For the first time in Jordan you can browse movie listings,timings as well as purchase tickets with a just a few clicks on your smart phone. All you have to do is download MARVEL mobile application on your phone now, choose the movie you want to watch, purchase your ticket, and head over to MARVEL Cinemas E-KIOSK to pick it up.

E-TICKETS

"Queuing at the box office" is so yesterday! Nowadays all cinemagoers have the luxury of booking their movie tickets online and with a click of a button. In 3 easy steps you will be good to go. Step one: choose the movie. Step two: pick your favorite seat. Step three: purchase your ticket. It's really that simple and at no extra cost.
